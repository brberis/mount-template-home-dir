Mobile Device Classroom Management Solution SPECIFICATIONS Project Description The Arlington ISD (AISD) is seeking a contract for cloud-based solutions to provide classroom teachers with real-time abilities to monitor and control student sessions on a variety of devices including Chromebooks, iPads, and both Windows 11 and MacOS computers. This award is “all or none.” Therefore, incomplete bids will not be considered. Questions Any questions regarding procurement or this bid should be submitted in writing via the electronic system, IonWave. All questions will be answered in writing and published in Ionwave (online bidding system) or via addendum through the same system. Telephone inquiries will not be accepted. Contacting other AISD personnel and/or members of the AISD Board of Trustees regarding this procurement is not allowed and may result in the offeror being disqualified from consideration for award. Purchase Order Requirements A valid purchase order from the AISD must be received before products are delivered, picked up, or services performed. Please note: If your company ships items prior to receiving a purchase order, the AISD is not responsible for paying your company for the products. All return shipping costs is the responsibility of your company. Contract Terms This bid will be effective from date of award (Board of Trustees Approval) for one year with the option to renew for two additional one-year periods. Payment Terms Payment will be made on the District’s regular payment schedule, net 30 days. Award Awards will be based on “best value to the district” in accordance with Texas Education Code, Section 44.031. Selection Criteria The following criteria will be used for selection: • 50% Section 1: Price • 30% Section 2: Functions • 10% Section 3: Support • 10% Section 4: References General Requirements The vendor shall tailor its bid to the circumstances and specifications outlined below. Vendor services unrelated to these specifications should not be included in responses. Arlington ISD is a large, urban public school district located in the geographic center of the Dallas-Ft. Worth metroplex. It serves approximately 51,000 students across grades Pre-K through 12. This service would be licensed for use in grades 2-12 which include ~41,000 students. The current minimum standard for student mobile devices is iPads for grades PK-1 and Chromebooks for grades 2-12. Additionally, Windows 11 and MacOS computers are in use in some specialized learning programs. Current releases of Windows 11, ChromeOS, iOS, and MacOS must be fully supported. For this RFP, all respondents must confirm the ability to deploy through device management solutions including but not limited to MECM, Jamf, InTune, and Google Admin Console and that the provided services are entirely cloud-based to allow for both on-site and off-site management. The following industry standards shall be adhered to unless specifically directed otherwise by the District. The list is not all-inclusive and does not alleviate compliance with the latest applicable standards, codes, and best practices: ● Industry Standard Network Protocols (TCP/IP, UDP) ● Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) ● Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) Scope of Work Requirements The scope of work for this RFP solicitation is specifically for cloud-based solutions that provide classroom teachers with real-time abilities to monitor and control student sessions on a variety of devices including Chromebooks, iPads, and both Windows 11 and MacOS computers. Required control and management functions at the classroom (teacher) level: ● Real-time access/visibility to student browsing activity in the active and background windows or tabs on the district-managed device ● Ability to easy set a recurring class schedule to automatically run ● Ability to “claim” a student who is erroneously engaged in another teacher’s class session ● Ability to record/capture what’s occurring on student devices ● Ability to retrieve students’ usage history and retrieve usage details ● Ability to lock/unlock and enable/disable screens/devices on one or more student devices (individual student, multiple students, entire class) ● Ability to force-open or push resources to student devices (website, for example) and lock student access to the teacher-pushed resource (individual student, multiple students, entire class) ● Ability to close or disable unapproved resources on the student’s device (individual student, multiple students, entire class) ● Ability to add manual blocks for non-instructional websites and save custom block lists ● Ability to communicate with one or more students ● Ability to receive automated notifications alerting teacher of one or more issues with student usage ● Ability for students to share their screens with the teacher and/or with other students with teacher’s approval ● Ability for the teacher to share their screen with students (individual student, multiple students, entire class) ● Ability for the teacher to customize their monitoring dashboard ● Ability to monitor and control students in both remote and F2F learning environments ● Ability to function equivalently on Chrome OS and Windows 11 devices (both Chrome and Edge browsers) ● Ability to easily manage class enrollment manually (add & remove both students and co-teachers) ● Ability to access help and live support resources via the teacher dashboard Preferred control and management functions: ● Ability for the teacher to take control of a student’s device ● Prominent notifications of system diagnostics (low battery, for example) and recommendations (updates, for example) ● In Windows environments, ability to block the use of specific browser(s) and/or software applications. ● In Windows environments, ability to monitor student activity across multiple monitors. Required district admin controls: ● Ability to edit / override classroom level settings and restrictions ● Ability to masquerade as teacher users ● Ability to manually create users and assign licenses ● Ability to create custom sets of block lists for teacher use ● Ability to investigate inappropriate student use via usage & history data and analytics ● Ability to track usage and analytics at district, school, grade, and class level Preferred district controls: ● Ability to integrate with the district’s internet filter solution (ContentKeeper) Other requirements: ● Single Sign On (SSO) via Classlink ● Class and student rostering via Classlink ● Macro-usage analytics and reporting to inform ROI ● Training resources, modules, etc. for teachers and admins ● Timely and direct troubleshooting support for both admin and end users (teachers) ● Comprehensive implementation support ● Deliverables: o Formal quote (41,000 student licenses) and formal quote (51,000 student licenses) o Privacy policy o Terms of Use / Service o Data Sharing Requirements o Account Provisioning & Password Management o Service-level Agreement (SLA) o Statement on Network Impact / Network Usage Data o Pilot opportunities o References from current public school system customers Additional Requirements In vendor’s proposals, please include pricing for single-year and multiple-year pricing.
